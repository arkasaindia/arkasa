Initiative

Research & Innovation Program

Empowering students, researchers, and innovators to transform ideas into impactful space research and real-world solutions.

The Research & Innovation Program is an initiative designed to support students, young professionals, and emerging researchers who are passionate about advancing knowledge in the space sector and interdisciplinary sciences.

Through this program, participants will receive mentorship, guidance, and collaborative opportunities to develop their ideas into structured research papers, prototypes, and real-world applications.

Our aim is to build a collaborative ecosystem of thinkers, designers, scientists, and engineers working at the intersection of space, technology, science, and creativity.

Participants will be guided through the process of research development, scientific writing, and publication, while also exploring possibilities to translate innovative ideas into practical prototypes and projects.

Disciplines

Fields of Research

We welcome proposals and research ideas in areas including (but not limited to):

Space Architecture
Aerospace Engineering
Space Biotechnology
Space Psychology & Human Factors
Planetary Habitat Design
Space Sustainability & Closed-loop Systems
Astrobiology
Advanced Materials for Space
Human–Space Interaction
Creative & Interdisciplinary Approaches

We also encourage cross-disciplinary ideas that combine science, design, engineering, and creative thinking.

Who Can Apply

  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Young professionals
  • Independent researchers
  • Innovators and creative thinkers
  • Anyone passionate about space-related research and innovation

No prior publication experience is required, curiosity and dedication to research are what matter most.

What Participants Will Gain

  • Learn how to write and structure a research paper
  • Receive mentorship from experienced researchers and professionals
  • Develop research proposals and scientific publications
  • Collaborate with a network of interdisciplinary researchers
  • Work toward prototyping innovative ideas
  • Contribute to future-focused space research initiatives
Process

Program Approach

The program will guide participants through key stages:

01

Idea Development & Research Direction

Define your research question and map out the direction and scope of your work alongside mentors.

02

Literature Review & Research Structuring

Survey existing knowledge and structure your research framework for clarity and academic rigour.

03

Scientific Writing & Documentation

Develop your findings into a well-documented paper with guidance on scientific writing standards.

04

Paper Submission & Publication Guidance

Receive support through the submission process, targeting relevant journals and conferences.

05

Prototype Development & Applied Research

Explore pathways to translate research insights into tangible prototypes and real-world applications.
