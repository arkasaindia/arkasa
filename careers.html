Careers at ARKASA

Join us in shaping the future of space architecture and extraterrestrial design.

At ARKASA, we are building a collaborative studio exploring the future of space architecture, extraterrestrial habitats, and interdisciplinary research.

Our work lies at the intersection of architecture, space science, design, simulation, and storytelling. We believe the future of space exploration will be shaped not just by engineers and scientists, but also by designers, thinkers, creators, and storytellers.

We are looking for passionate individuals who are curious about space and eager to learn, collaborate, and experiment with new ideas.

All positions currently offered are remote and voluntary (unpaid) and are intended for individuals who want to gain experience, contribute to research initiatives, and be part of a growing creative space-tech studio.

Why Join ARKASA?

Work on future-focused space concepts
Collaborate with researchers, designers, and engineers
Gain portfolio-worthy experience
Be part of an experimental creative studio exploring space futures

3D Game Developer Intern

Remote, Voluntary
+

As a 3D Game Developer Intern, you will work on developing interactive simulation environments and visual tools that support our research on extraterrestrial habitats and space systems.

Responsibilities

  • Developing interactive 3D/2D simulations, prototypes, and visualization tools supporting ARKASA's extraterrestrial habitat research
  • Implementing gameplay mechanics, physics systems, and environment interactions
  • Assisting in procedural generation and simulation of extraterrestrial terrains
  • Optimizing assets, lighting, animation, and performance for smooth execution
  • Collaborating with architects, designers, and researchers to translate conceptual models into simulation environments
  • Debugging and refining game systems for stability and accuracy
